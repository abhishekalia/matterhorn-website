9 Types of Coverage Tailored for Pickleball

Our specialized program provides complete protection designed specifically for pickleball organizations, facilities, and events. Each coverage type addresses the unique risks you face.

General Liability

Protection against third-party claims for bodily injury, property damage, and personal injury occurring on your premises or during activities.

Accident Medical

Coverage for immediate medical expenses resulting from accidents during pickleball activities, regardless of fault.

Property Coverage

Protection for your facility, equipment, and personal property against fire, theft, vandalism, and other covered perils.

Commercial Facility

Specialized coverage for commercial pickleball facilities including business interruption and additional living expenses.

E&O / D&O

Professional liability protection for management decisions and coverage for directors and officers against lawsuits.

Crime / Cyber

Protection against employee dishonesty, theft, and cyber security breaches including data recovery and notification costs.

Alcohol Liability

Coverage for incidents related to serving or selling alcoholic beverages at events and tournaments.

Abuse & Molestation

Specialized coverage for claims involving abuse or molestation by staff, volunteers, or participants.

Registration Refund

Protection for member registration fees in case of event cancellation, facility closure, or other covered circumstances.
